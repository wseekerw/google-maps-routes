This is the routes page where you can save and manage your routes. Created routes are saved directly into the database so you can access them whenever you login into an account.

To create a route you need to fill the starting point and the endpoint field and click on "Submit Destination" button. Both of those forms have an autocomplete function which helps you to accurately pinpoint the locations. Once you've created a route click on it to open a map and to see the destination and travel time.

This site calculates the destination and travel time by car only.

* Be careful while entering the starting and ending destination. If you fail to submit a full name of the city or town then your route won't be properly created.
